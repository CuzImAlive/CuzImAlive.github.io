Biomedical Engineering Student · CWRU

I'm a Biomedical Engineering student at Case Western Reserve University, broadly interested in neural engineering, electrophysiology, and non-pharmacological approaches to pain management.

Background

My research sits at the intersection of photobiomodulation and neural inhibition, with a focus on selectively targeting nociceptive C-fibers as an alternative to opioid-based pain management. I work in the Moffitt Lab at CWRU under Dr. Mike Moffitt and Dr. Aaron Jenkins.

I'm currently applying to MD-PhD (MSTP) programs, with the goal of combining a career in neurosurgery with continued research in neural engineering and neurotech. I've had the opportunity to work at the Mayo Clinic SURF program under Dr. Kendall Lee, an experience that deepened my commitment to translational research.

Outside the lab, I'm a classically trained oboist and enjoy thinking about entrepreneurship, investing, and how technology shapes medicine.
